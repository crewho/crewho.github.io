About

Crewho is an app that gives you the power to break the boundaries of the creative industry. Instead of waiting for opportunities, our app allows you to create them by directly contacting creators like you who are desperately looking for a chance to show their true potential.

Features

Crewho has a very powerful search algorithm that will help you find creators based on their skillsets, location, and willingness to work.

Crewho understands the skillsets you are looking for in a fellow creator and places these suggested creators in your homepage so that you can foster connections with them in hopes of starting a project in the future.
